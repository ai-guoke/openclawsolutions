What is OpenClaw? How is it different from ChatGPT? OpenClaw 是什么？和 ChatGPT 有什么区别？

OpenClaw is an open-source AI Agent runtime platform. If ChatGPT is a "mouth" (can only chat), OpenClaw gives AI a pair of "hands" and "eyes" — it reads emails, manages calendars, browses the web, executes commands, and works proactively 24/7. All data stays on your own server.

OpenClaw 是一个开源的 AI Agent 运行平台。如果说 ChatGPT 是一个「嘴」（只能聊天），OpenClaw 就是给 AI 大脑配了一双「手」和一双「眼睛」——它能读邮件、管日历、上网搜索、操作浏览器、执行命令，24 小时不间断主动为你工作。所有数据都在你自己的服务器上，完全私有。

Is my enterprise data safe? 我的企业数据安全吗？

Absolutely. We use private deployment with Docker sandbox isolation, Composio OAuth middleware, and multi-layer firewall hardening. Your data never leaves your own infrastructure.
